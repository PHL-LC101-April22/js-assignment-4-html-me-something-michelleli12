What I Did in Las Vegas

  • ate lots of good food
  • horseback riding
  • saw the Beatles LOVE Cirque du Soleil show
  • went on some tours

Food I Ate

I went to Wing Lei & Sinatra at the Wynn, Catch & Din Tai Fung at Aria, Sushi Roku in the Forum Shops at Caesar's Palace, and the Bacchanal buffet in Caesar's palace.

Horseback Riding

I went on a 2 hour horseback ride through Red Rocks Canyon. My horse's name was Rose and she was great. We saw amazing views and rode right next to the edge of a cliff.

The Beatles LOVE

I got to see the Beatles LOVE Cirque du Soleil show. It was an amazing show! I've always wanted to see a Cirque due Soleil show and it did not disappoint.

Tours I Went On

  • Pink Jeep Tour through Red Rocks Canyon
  • Horseshoe Bend
  • Antelope Canyon
